Review & Strategy

Pixel Jumper taps into the pure, addictive nature of the endless runner genre. There's no complex plot, only a simple, compelling goal: survive. The game demands rhythm and precision, and mastering the double jump is the key to seeing your score climb into the stratosphere. It's the perfect game to test your reflexes in short, intense bursts.

Tips for a High Score

  • Master the Double Jump: Don't use your double jump impulsively. Save it for when you truly need it to cross a wide gap or correct a mis-timed initial jump.
  • Look Ahead: Don't just focus on the platform you're about to land on. Keep an eye on the next one or two platforms to plan your trajectory in advance.
  • Find Your Rhythm: The game has a steady pace. Try to get into a rhythmic flow of jumping, which can help with consistency and prevent premature jumps.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a double jump?

A double jump allows you to jump a second time while in mid-air. In Pixel Jumper, press the Spacebar once to jump from a platform, and press it again while in the air to get an extra boost to reach a faraway platform.

How is the score calculated?

The score in Pixel Jumper is based on survival time. The longer you successfully jump between platforms without falling, the higher your score will be.
