A high-speed test of your dodging skills.

Review & Strategy

Asteroid Dodger distills the excitement of space survival into a pure, arcade-style experience. There are no power-ups or special abilities—it's just you, your ship, and an endless barrage of cosmic rock. The game is a fantastic measure of reaction time and the ability to find safe paths through chaos. Its simple controls make it instantly accessible, while the ever-increasing speed provides a steep and satisfying challenge.

Survival Tips

  • Small, Controlled Movements: Avoid making wide, sweeping movements from one side of the screen to the other. Make small, precise adjustments to navigate through gaps.
  • Stay Near the Center: While it seems counter-intuitive, staying closer to the center gives you more options to move left or right to dodge an obstacle. Hugging the walls can easily get you trapped.
  • Look High: Focus your eyes on the top third of the screen where new asteroids appear. This gives you the maximum amount of time to react and plan your route.
