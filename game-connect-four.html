Connect Four

The classic vertical strategy game for two players.

Player 1's Turn (Red)

Review & Strategy

Connect Four takes the simple concept of Tic-Tac-Toe and adds a new dimension: gravity. This single change transforms it into a game of deep strategic possibilities. Planning ahead is crucial, as every piece you drop not only advances your own plan but also potentially blocks your opponent or, conversely, sets them up for a future win. It's a perfect introductory strategy game for all ages.

Winning Strategies

  • Control the Center: The player who controls the center column has a significant advantage, as it's part of the most potential winning lines.
  • Play Offensively: Proactively try to create multiple threats (rows where you have three discs and two open spots to win). This forces your opponent into defensive moves and gives you control of the game's tempo.
  • Think Ahead: Before you make a move, consider your opponent's potential responses. Don't make a move that allows them to win on their next turn.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is it better to go first in Connect Four?

Yes. Connect Four is a 'solved game', which means that if the first player plays perfectly, they can always force a win. However, for casual human players, the advantage is very slight.

What is the most basic Connect Four strategy?

A fundamental strategy is to control the center column. A line of four can be formed through the center column in more ways than any other column, so controlling it is a major advantage. Always be on the lookout to create multiple threats at once.
